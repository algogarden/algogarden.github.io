Still, like air, I rise.
Long showers, loud music,
deep thoughts.
When I smile,
the word smiles with me.
Happiness can exist only in
acceptance.
Motivation is when your
dreams put on work clothes.
Into each life some rain
must fall.
I’ve always loved the night,
when everyone else is asleep and
the world is all mine
I want to be wild and free just
like the sea.
I'm a house of cards in your
hurricane.
There is no friend as loyal
as a book.
We were just young, dumb
and broke.
Joy multiplies when it is
shared among friends.
What’s essential is invisible
to the eyes.
Every summer, like the roses,
childhood returns.
What sunshine is to flowers,
smiles are to humanity.
